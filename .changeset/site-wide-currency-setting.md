Add a site-wide default currency setting. Admins can now choose the currency (EUR, USD, GBP, CHF, DKK, NOK, SEK, PLN, CZK, HUF) in Fair Payments Connector → Settings → Currency; all new transactions, fees, and price displays across the plugins inherit this setting instead of being hard-coded to EUR.
